Stask

I developed Stask as a fast and simple solution to organize daily to-do's.

It is build for
  • superfast keyboard only use,
  • utilizing System-wide hotkeys to invoke the tool from every window.
  • releasing focus and giving it back to the last window

Stasks won't interrupt your workflow, it integrates into it.
